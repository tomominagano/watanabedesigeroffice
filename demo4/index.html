ABOUT US

A house is where you live every day for a long time. we want the house to have a deeper taste as the years go by than when it was completed. we are aiming for material selection and shape that will not get tired of, rather than a design that is too new. we specialize in floor plans that allow you to feel the signs of your family (such as plaster and solid materials), which are natural materials rather than industrialized products and do not require a catalog. it would be a great pleasure to have a building that would be the original scenery of the children who grew up there.
